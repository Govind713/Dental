Oral Surgery & Dental Implants

Comprehensive surgical care including extractions, implant placement and same-day restorations where appropriate.

Our surgical services

  • Simple and surgical tooth extractions
  • Immediate and delayed implant placement
  • Bone grafting and ridge preservation
  • Sinus lifts and advanced implant planning
  • Comfort options: local anaesthesia, sedation where needed

We plan implant cases with digital radiography and guided surgery for predictable outcomes. Our restorative team coordinates implant crowns and bridges to restore both function and esthetics.

What to expect

We conduct a thorough clinical and radiographic assessment, discuss options, risks and expected recovery. We provide written post-operative instructions and a follow-up plan to ensure healing and successful restoration.
