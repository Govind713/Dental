Our Services

Complete dental care with modern techniques and compassionate service.

General Dentistry

Led by Dr. Anoopkumar Ravindranath, our general dentistry services focus on prevention, accurate diagnosis, and long-term oral health. From routine dental check-ups to essential treatments, every step is guided by clarity and ethical care.

Orthodontics & Clear Aligners

Headed by Dr. Terry Thomas Edathotty, we provide orthodontic care for children, adolescents, and adults, including advanced clear aligner treatments for discreet and effective smile correction.

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery Including Laser Treatments

All oral surgical procedures are managed by Dr. Justin Mathew, Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon. We also offer advanced laser-assisted dental treatments, which allow for precise, minimally invasive procedures with faster healing and improved comfort.

Root Canal Treatments (Endodontics)

Root canal treatments are handled by a dedicated team of experienced Endodontists, ensuring accurate, comfortable, and efficient care:

  • Dr. Renjithraj
  • Dr. Sijo P. Mathew
  • Dr. Shibhu Sreedhar

Periodontics & Implantology

Under the care of Dr. Joseph J. Pulikottil, we offer specialized treatments for gum health and dental implants, helping restore both function and confidence with precision and care.

Pediatric Dentistry Interceptive & Myofunctional Treatments

Specialized dental care for children is provided by Paedodontist Dr. Krishnakumar R, focusing on early intervention, growth guidance, and gentle, child-friendly treatment approaches.

Smile Designing

Aesthetic and smile enhancement treatments are led by Dr. Shibhu Sreedhar, with personalized planning to create natural, confident, and lasting smiles.

Complete Dental Care Under One Roof

From routine care to advanced specialty treatments, Anupam Dental Clinic provides comprehensive dental solutions in one place, guided by trust, safety, and ethical practice.
